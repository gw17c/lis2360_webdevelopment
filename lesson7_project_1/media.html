Most Popular Devices:

Amazon Echo Amazon Echo, the connected voice controller from Amazon, can give you information, music, news, weather, add things to todo lists, wake you up and, of course, control your home.
ecobee4 The ecobee has the Alexa service built-in to tell it to change the temperature, set a timer, read you an article, etc. The device comes with a temperature and humidity sensor.
Rachio Smart Sprinkler Controller 2nd Gen This sprinkler system automatically adjusts according to the weather. It also has WiFi connectivity that can give alerts when the schedule changes.
Bitdefender BOX - IoT Security Solution Security solution which blocks incoming threats and can scan all your devices for vulnerabilities. It protects all your IoT devices and can act as a wireless router or go alongside your current one.
Google Home Voice controller Google Home, the connected voice controller from Google. Besides controlling your home it also comes with Google Assistant, helping with lists, translation, news, music, calendar and many many more.
Nest Cam Indoor camera Nest Cam Outdoor is the monitoring tool you've been waiting for. It brings all the benefits of modern streaming technology and a sleek design so you can watch your home from anywhere.
